Francesco Di Filippo

About Me

Hello, my name is Francesco and I am from Salerno, a beautiful city in the south of Italy.
I obtained my Ph.D. in astroparticle physics under the supervision of Stefano Liberati in SISSA, Trieste, Italy.
After that, I moved to Kyoto, Prague, and I am now in Frankfurt.
I am interested in several aspects of black hole physics, including semiclassical effects and the singularity problem. You can fing the full list of my pubblications on my Inspire page.
There are many things I like to do when I am not doing physics. Among them are cooking and playing chess. If you like to play chess too, we can do it together here.
